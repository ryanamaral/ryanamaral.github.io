#### WatchMiner - AntMiner Monitor

Monitor your ASIC Miner in real-time!

Monitor:
* Hashrate
* Frequency
* Temperatures of Chip & PCB (Celsius / Fahrenheit)
* FAN speed
* ASIC health status
* Hardware errors
* Pool stats (Found blocks, Difficulty, Last share, etc)
* Network
* Monitor & Kernel Logs

Management:
* Reboot your AntMiner
